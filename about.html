About PCSSS

Who We Are

Purvanchal Computer Shikshan Seva Sanstha is a leading Skills and Talent Development Corporation that is building a manpower pool for global institute. The company, which was set up in 2008 to help the nascent IT industry overcome its human resource challenges, today ranks among the world's leading training companies owing to its vast, yet comprehensive array of talent development programs. With a footprint across 40 nations, PCSSS offers training and development solutions to Individuals, Enterprises and Institutions.

We actively participate in Government Scheme Training Programs such as PMGDISHA and state-level skill development missions to offer subsidized or free training to eligible students.

Welcome to Purvanchal Computer Shikshan Seva Sanstha

Established in 2008, Purvanchal Computer Shikshan Seva Sanstha (PCSSS) Limited, a global leader in Skills and Talent Development, offers multi-disciplinary learning management and training delivery solutions to corporations and institutions. It has become known, in particular, as a corporate training company and Industrial Training Company, with customized programs for both large and small enterprises.

PCSSS has three main lines of business across the globe: Corporate Learning Group, Skills and Careers Group, and the School Learning Group and PMKVY Skill development courses.

Corporate Learning Group (CLG)

PCSSS Corporate Learning Group (CLG) has established PCSSS as a leading Corporate Training Institute which offers Managed Training Services (MTS) to market-leading organizations in Delhi NCR. The comprehensive suite of Managed Training Services includes custom Curriculum Design and Content Development, Learning Administration, Learning Delivery, Strategic Sourcing, Learning Technology and Advisory Services. With a team of some of the world's finest learning professionals, PCSSS is dedicated to helping customers increase the business value of learning and development (L&D). Built on the sound principles of 'Running Training like a Business', PCSSS's Managed Training Services and best-in-class training processes enable customers to align business goals with L&D, reduce costs, realize measurable value, benefit from rock-solid operations and increase business impact.

Skills and Careers Group (SCG)

PCSSS's Skills and Careers Group (SCG) delivers a diverse range of learning and talent development programs to millions of individuals and corporate learners in areas including Banking, Finance & Insurance, Soft Skills, Business Analytics, Retail Sales Enablement, Management Education, Vocational Skills, Digital Media Marketing and new-age IT. These programs are delivered through a hybrid combination of the 'Cloud Campus' online platform, satellite-based 'Synchronous Learning Technology', and a physical network of hundreds of learning centers around the globe. The flagship multi-disciplinary course offerings include the industry-endorsed IT program, a set of Post Graduate Programs for students from different streams and a wide range of specialist short duration programs.

Global Partnerships & Recognition

To further strengthen its SCG portfolio in India, PCSSS has tied up with industry majors like Strickview technology Pvt Ltd for IFBI; leading business schools in India for PCSSS Imperia; Genpact for PCSSS Uniqua; and a joint venture with NSDC for PCSSS Yuva Jyoti. Besides this, for the China market, PCSSS has tied up with governments courses. PCSSS's learning and talent development solutions have received widespread recognition globally. PCSSS was recognized as the Best Vocational Education and Skill Development initiative at the World Education Summit, 2008.
