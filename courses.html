Our Courses

Empowering you to |

O Level (NIELIT)

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: 10+2 / ITI Certificate

A comprehensive foundation course in Computer Applications. Covers IT tools, Web Designing (HTML, CSS, JS), Python Programming, and Internet of Things (IoT).

CCC

  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Eligibility: 10th Pass

Course on Computer Concepts. Highly recommended and often mandatory for various state government clerical jobs. Covers basic office automation and internet skills.

ADCA

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: 10th / 12th Pass

Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications. Make yourself industry-ready. Includes advanced Office Automation, Financial Accounting (Tally Prime), and Desktop Publishing (DTP).

DCA

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Eligibility: 10th Pass

Diploma in Computer Applications. Perfect for beginners to gain a solid operational knowledge of computers, Windows OS, MS Office, and basic internet browsing.

ASIM

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: 10th / 12th Pass

Advanced Diploma in Software Installation. Learn complete PC setup, operating system installation, troubleshooting, and essential software configuration.

DTP (Desktop Publishing)

  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Eligibility: 10th Pass

Master professional design software like Photoshop, CorelDRAW, and PageMaker to create stunning graphics, print layouts, and digital publications.

CAM (Computer Aided Manufacturing)

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Eligibility: 10th / 12th / ITI

Introduction to modern manufacturing processes. Learn to use computer software to control machine tools and related machinery in the manufacturing industry.
